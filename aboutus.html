~About AVSTech~

and in just over two decades has established a successful presence
across the portfolio of construction business verticals.

Concrete Blocks and
RMC business from strength to strength since then.

has established itself as a major building material supplier
and continues to expand and diversify.

Managing Director, a business development professional,
